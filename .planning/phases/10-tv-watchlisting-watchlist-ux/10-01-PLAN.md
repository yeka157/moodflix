Purpose: Every downstream consumer (TV detail page, library cards, movie cards on series page) depends on the hooks returning the right watchlist state per media type. Without this, adding a TV show could incorrectly light up a movie card with the same TMDB ID.

Output: Updated hooks, server actions, and card components with mediaType threading.
</objective>
