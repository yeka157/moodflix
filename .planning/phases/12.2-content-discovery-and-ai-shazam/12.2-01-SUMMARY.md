## Decisions Made
- Fire-and-forget cache writes with `.catch(() => {})` to avoid latency on page loads
- Category cache key format `{category}_{mediaType}_{page}` for unique per-page entries
- voteAverage and popularity stored as text in tmdb_media to avoid floating-point precision issues
- Detail cache is permanent; ratings TTL (7 days) exported for future use by subsequent plans
