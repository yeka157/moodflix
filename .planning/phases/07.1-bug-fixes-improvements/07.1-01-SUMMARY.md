# Plan 07.1-01 Summary: AI & Grid Fixes

## What was done
1. **AI model upgrade**: Switched from retired `gemini-2.0-flash` to `gemini-2.5-flash-lite` in `lib/ai.ts`
2. **Quota error handling**: Added `maxRetries: 0` to prevent silent retry on 429/503, `onError` logging, and recursive `isQuotaOrRateLimitError()` detection that returns a user-friendly 503 response
3. **Grid orphan fix**: `MovieGrid` now accepts `(Movie | null)[]` and renders null items as invisible placeholder divs. `SeriesGridContent` and `DiscoverContent` pad their arrays to nearest multiple of 6 when all pages are loaded

## Files modified
- `lib/ai.ts` — model string change
- `app/api/ai/recommend/route.ts` — maxRetries, onError, quota detection + 503 response
- `components/movies/movie-grid.tsx` — nullable movie support with placeholder divs
- `components/series/series-grid-content.tsx` — paddedShows useMemo
- `components/movies/discover-content.tsx` — paddedSearchMovies + paddedGenreMovies useMemo

## Decisions
- Used `gemini-2.5-flash-lite` (1000 RPD free tier) over `gemini-2.5-flash` (lower free quota)
- Pad to multiple of 6 (xl breakpoint column count) — covers all breakpoints since 2/3/4/5 all divide into 6's multiples at small counts
